Title: Pina Colada Smoothie (Vegan) Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1898183.jpg
Rating: 4.5
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 3.0 cubes  ice cubes, or as needed
Ingredient: 1.0 banana
Ingredient: 1.0 cup fresh pineapple chunks
Ingredient: 0.5 cup coconut milk
Ingredient: 0.5 cup soy milk
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon agave nectar
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon ground flax seed
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend ice, banana, pineapple, coconut milk, soy milk, agave nectar, flax seed, and vanilla extract in a blender until smooth. Pour smoothie into a tall glass.
