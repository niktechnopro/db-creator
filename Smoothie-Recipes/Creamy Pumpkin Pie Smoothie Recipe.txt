Title: Creamy Pumpkin Pie Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4976836.jpg
Rating: 5.0
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 frozen banana
Ingredient: 0.75 cup plain Greek yogurt
Ingredient: 0.5 cup almond milk
Ingredient: 0.5 cup ice, or as desired
Ingredient: 0.25 cup pumpkin puree
Ingredient: 0.5 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend banana, yogurt, almond milk, ice, pumpkin puree, and pumpkin pie spice together in a blender until smooth.
