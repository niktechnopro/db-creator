Title: Mango-Peach Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/414770.jpg
Rating: 4.66666650772095
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 peach, sliced
Ingredient: 1.0 mango, peeled and diced
Ingredient: 0.5 cup vanilla soy milk
Ingredient: 0.5 cup orange juice, or as needed
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Place the peach, mango, soy milk, and orange juice into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth. Pour into glasses to serve.
