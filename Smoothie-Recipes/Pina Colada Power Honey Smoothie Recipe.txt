Title: Pina Colada Power Honey Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 0.5 cup water
Ingredient: 0.5 cup reduced-fat coconut milk
Ingredient: 0.5 cup pineapple chunks
Ingredient: 0.5 cup ice cubes
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon honey, or more to taste
Ingredient: 1.0 scoop vanilla whey protein powder
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Combine water, coconut milk, pineapple chunks, ice, honey, and vanilla protein powder in a blender. Blend until smooth, 1 to 2 minutes.
