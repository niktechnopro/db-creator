Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 0.25 cup orange juice, or to taste

Ingredient: 1.0 frozen banana, sliced

Ingredient: 1.0 ounce) container vanilla yogurt
Ingredient: 2.0 ice cubes, or as desired
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon ground ginger
Directions: 
Duration: 15 m
Step: Pour orange juice into the pitcher of a blender and add mango, frozen banana, carrots, vanilla yogurt, ice cubes, and ginger. Pulse several times to crush ice, then blend until smooth, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
