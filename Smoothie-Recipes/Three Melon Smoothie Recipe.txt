Title: Three Melon Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/249415.jpg
Rating: 3.33333325386047
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 2.0 cups seedless watermelon, cubed
Ingredient: 1.0 cup honeydew melon, cubed
Ingredient: 1.0 cup cantaloupe melon, cubed
Ingredient: 0.5 cup ice cubes
Ingredient: 0.5 cup lemon-lime soda pop
Directions: 
Duration: 15 m
Step: Place the watermelon, honeydew, cantaloupe, and ice in a blender.  Pour in the lemon-lime soda and puree until smooth.
