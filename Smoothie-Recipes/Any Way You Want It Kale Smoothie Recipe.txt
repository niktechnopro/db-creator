Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 2.0 cups chopped kale
Ingredient: 0.25 cup Greek yogurt
Ingredient: 3.0 tablespoons peanuts (optional)
Ingredient: 0.5 cup milk
Ingredient: 0.5 frozen banana
Ingredient: 3.0 frozen strawberries, or more to taste
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon maple syrup
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
