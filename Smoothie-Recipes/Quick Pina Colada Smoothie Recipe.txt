Title: Quick Pina Colada Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5024804.jpg
Rating: 4.0
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 cup pineapple juice
Ingredient: 0.5 cup unsweetened coconut cream
Ingredient: 0.25 cup ice cubes, or as desired
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend pineapple juice, coconut cream, and ice cubes together in a blender on high speed until smooth, 30 to 40 seconds.
