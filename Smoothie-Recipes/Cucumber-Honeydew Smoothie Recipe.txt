Title: Cucumber-Honeydew Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/219998.jpg
Rating: 4.307692527771
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 cucumber, peeled, seeded and chopped
Ingredient: 2.0 cups cubed honeydew melon
Ingredient: 2.0 cups passion fruit juice
Ingredient: 8.0 sprigs fresh mint, or amount to taste
Ingredient: 2.0 cups crushed ice
Directions: 
Duration: 15 m
Step: Combine cucumber, honeydew melon, passion fruit juice, and ice in a blender. Pull leaves from mint springs and add to blender. Blend mixture until smooth. Pour into tall glasses.
