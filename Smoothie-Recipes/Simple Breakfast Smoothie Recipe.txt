Title: Simple Breakfast Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2053863.jpg
Rating: 4.19999980926514
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 cup yogurt
Ingredient: 1.0 orange, peeled and broken into sections
Ingredient: 1.0 banana, broken into chunks
Ingredient: 0.25 cup strawberries, or more to taste
Ingredient: 0.25 cup ice cubes, or as needed
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon vanilla extract
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon ground flax seeds (optional)
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend yogurt, orange, banana, strawberries, ice, vanilla extract, and flax seeds together in a blender until smooth.
