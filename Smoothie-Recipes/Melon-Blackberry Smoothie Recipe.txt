Title: Melon-Blackberry Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/962948.jpg
Rating: 4.5
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 banana, chopped
Ingredient: 0.5 honeydew melon, cubed
Ingredient: 0.5 cup frozen blackberries
Ingredient: 3.0 tablespoons wheat germ
Ingredient: 0.75 cup almond milk
Ingredient: 6.0 ice cubes
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Place the banana, honeydew melon, blackberries, wheat germ, and almond milk into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth.
Step: Divide ice cubes into two tall glasses, and pour the smoothie over the ice.
