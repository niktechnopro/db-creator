Title: Honey-Mango Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/506492.jpg
Rating: 2.78571438789368
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 mango - peeled, seeded, and cubed
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon white sugar
Ingredient: 2.0 tablespoons honey
Ingredient: 1.0 cup nonfat milk
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon lemon juice
Ingredient: 1.0 cup ice cubes
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Place the mango, sugar, and honey in a blender pitcher; pour in the milk and lemon juice, and blend until smooth. Divide the ice cubes between two serving glasses. Pour the mango smoothie over ice to serve.
