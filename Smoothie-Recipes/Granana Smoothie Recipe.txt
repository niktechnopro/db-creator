Title: Granana Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 0.5 cup 2% milk 
Ingredient: 1.0 Granny Smith apple - peeled, cored, and cut into chunks
Ingredient: 1.0 banana, cut up
Ingredient: 2.0 ice cubes
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend the milk, apple, banana, and ice cubes in a blender until smooth; divide between 2 glasses to serve.
