Title: BFF Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5029498.jpg
Rating: 4.2857141494751
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 cup frozen strawberries
Ingredient: 1.0 cup plain Greek yogurt
Ingredient: 1.0 cup frozen mango chunks
Ingredient: 0.5 cup 1% milk
Ingredient: 1.0 scoop vanilla whey protein powder
Ingredient: 5.0 fresh mint leaves, or more to taste
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend strawberries, Greek yogurt, mango, milk, and protein powder together until smooth. Add mint leaves and pulse until leaves are chopped, 4 to 5 pulses.
