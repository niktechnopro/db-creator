Title: Peachy Keen Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/228845.jpg
Rating: 3.33333325386047
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 cup sliced peaches
Ingredient: 4.0 cubes ice (optional)
Ingredient: 0.25 teaspoon ground nutmeg
Ingredient: 0.5 teaspoon vanilla extract
Ingredient: 0.5 teaspoon honey
Ingredient: 2.0 teaspoons wheat germ
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon rolled oats
Ingredient: 1.0 cup vanilla soy milk
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Place the peaches, ice, nutmeg, vanilla extract, honey, wheat germ, oats, and soy milk into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth. Pour into glasses to serve.
