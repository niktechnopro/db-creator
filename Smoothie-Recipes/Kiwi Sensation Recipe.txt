Title: Kiwi Sensation Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/950817.jpg
Rating: 4.0
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 kiwi, peeled and sliced
Ingredient: 0.5 cup pineapple chunks
Ingredient: 0.5 cup green grapes
Ingredient: 0.25 mango - peeled, seeded, and diced
Ingredient: 0.25 cup water
Ingredient: 0.5 cup spinach
Ingredient: 2.0 cubes ice cubes, or as needed
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend kiwi, pineapple, grapes, mango, and water in a blender until smooth. Add spinach and blend again until spinach is completely integrated. Add ice and blend until ice is completely integrated.

Servings: 2
