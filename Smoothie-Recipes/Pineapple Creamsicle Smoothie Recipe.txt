Title: Pineapple Creamsicle Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 0.5 fresh pineapple - peeled, cored, and chopped
Ingredient: 1.0 cup vanilla yogurt
Ingredient: 1.0 banana, cut into chunks
Ingredient: 0.5 cup orange juice
Ingredient: 32.0 ice cubes, or as needed
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend pineapple, yogurt, banana, and orange juice together in a blender until smooth. Add ice and blend until ice is completely crushed and incorporated.
