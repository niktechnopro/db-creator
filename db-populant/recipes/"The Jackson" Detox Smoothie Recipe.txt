Title: "The Jackson" Detox Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/global/recipes/nophoto/nopicture-910x511.png
Rating: 0.0
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 cups frozen strawberries, divided
Ingredient: 1.0 cups soy milk
Ingredient: 0.5 cup frozen blueberries
Ingredient: 2.0 large scoops acai powder
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon flaxseed oil
Ingredient: 2.0 teaspoons stevia powder
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Place 1 cup strawberries in a blender; blend on ice crush setting until smooth. Add the remaining 1/2 cup strawberries, soy milk, and blueberries; blend until well combined. Add acai powder, flaxseed oil, and stevia with a spoon. Blend until smooth.
