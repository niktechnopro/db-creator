Title: Nutty Avocado Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3550969.jpg
Rating: 4.75
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon shelled pistachios
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon almonds
Ingredient: 0.75 cup unsweetened coconut milk
Ingredient: 5.0 cubes ice cubes
Ingredient: 0.5 large avocado, peeled and pitted
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon honey
Ingredient: 1.0 pinch saffron threads (optional)
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend pistachios and almonds in a blender until ground; add coconut milk, ice cubes, avocado, honey, and saffron. Blend until smooth.
