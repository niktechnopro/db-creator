Title: Vanilla-Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/812453.jpg
Rating: 3.29999995231628
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 2.0 bananas, broken into chunks
Ingredient: 1.0 cup vanilla ice cream
Ingredient: 0.5 teaspoon vanilla extract
Ingredient: 0.5 cup fresh orange juice
Ingredient: 1.0 cup milk
Directions: 
Duration: 2 m
Step: Place banana and vanilla ice cream into a blender. Pour in vanilla extract, orange juice, and milk. Puree until thick and smooth.
