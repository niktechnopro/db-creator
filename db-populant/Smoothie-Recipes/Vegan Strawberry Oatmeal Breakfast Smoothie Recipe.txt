Title: Vegan Strawberry Oatmeal Breakfast Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 cup almond milk
Ingredient: 0.5 cup rolled oats
Ingredient: 14.0 frozen strawberries
Ingredient: 1.0 banana, broken into chunks
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoons agave nectar (optional)
Ingredient: 0.5 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend almond milk, oats, strawberries, banana, agave nectar, and vanilla extract in a blender until smooth.
