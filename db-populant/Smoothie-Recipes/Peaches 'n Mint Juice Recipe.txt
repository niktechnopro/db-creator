Title: Peaches 'n Mint Juice Recipe

Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 3.0 large peaches or nectarines, cubed
Ingredient: 1.0 large apple, quartered
Ingredient: 1.0 lime
Ingredient: 2.0 sprigs fresh mint
Directions: 
Duration: 5 m
Step: Juice the peaches, apple, and lime with a juice extractor.  Transfer the juice to a blender with the mint leaves.  Blend quickly to combine the juice and mint thoroughly.
