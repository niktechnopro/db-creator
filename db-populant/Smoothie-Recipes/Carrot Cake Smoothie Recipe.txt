Title: Carrot Cake Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1123828.jpg
Rating: 4.0
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 large carrot, peeled and diced
Ingredient: 0.25 cup frozen mango chunks
Ingredient: 1.0 large fresh peach, chopped
Ingredient: 0.25 cup soy milk
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon ground cinnamon
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon ground allspice
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon ground ginger
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Pulse carrot and mango together in a blender until roughly chopped. Add peach, soy milk, cinnamon, allspice, and ginger; blend until smooth.
