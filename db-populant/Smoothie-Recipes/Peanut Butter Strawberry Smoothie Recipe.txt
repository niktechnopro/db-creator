Title: Peanut Butter Strawberry Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 8.0 strawberries, sliced
Ingredient: 0.5 cup plain yogurt
Ingredient: 0.25 cup milk
Ingredient: 2.0 tablespoons smooth peanut butter
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon brown sugar
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend strawberries, yogurt, milk, peanut butter, and brown sugar together in a blender for 2 minutes; let rest for 10 seconds. Blend until smooth, another 2 minutes.
