Title: Apple Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/394662.jpg

Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 frozen bananas, peeled and chopped
Ingredient: 0.5 cup orange juice
Ingredient: 1.0 Gala apple, peeled, cored and chopped
Ingredient: 0.25 cup milk
Directions: 
Duration: 5 m
Step: In a blender combine frozen banana, orange juice, apple and milk. Blend until smooth. pour into glasses and serve.
