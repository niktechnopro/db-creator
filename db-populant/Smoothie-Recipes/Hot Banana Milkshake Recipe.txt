Title: Hot Banana Milkshake Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4478103.jpg
Rating: 3.63636374473572
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 2.0 small ripe bananas, chopped
Ingredient: 0.25 teaspoon brown sugar
Ingredient: 1.0 cups milk, divided
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon honey
Ingredient: 0.125 teaspoon ground cinnamon
Directions: 
Duration: 15 m
Step: Mash bananas and brown sugar together in a bowl. Transfer to a small saucepan, place over medium heat, and cook until bananas begin to brown, about 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Gradually mix 1 cup milk into bananas and heat until mixture begins to steam. Stir honey and cinnamon into mixture until honey has dissolved.
Step: Pour banana mixture into a blender no more than half full. Cover and hold lid down; pulse a few times before leaving on to blend. Puree until smooth. Blend remaining 1/2 cup milk into mixture until creamy.
