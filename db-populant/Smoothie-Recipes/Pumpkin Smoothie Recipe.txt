Title: Pumpkin Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 ounce) can pumpkin puree
Ingredient: 2.0 cups milk
Ingredient: 0.25 cup brown sugar
Ingredient: 2.0 teaspoons ground cinnamon
Directions: 
Duration: 5 m
Step: Place the pumpkin puree in a freezer bag; store in freezer for at least 24 hours.
Step: Heat the bag of pumpkin puree in the microwave on HIGH to soften, 1 to 2 minutes.
Step: Pour the milk into a blender. Add the brown sugar, cinnamon, and pumpkin; blend until smooth.
