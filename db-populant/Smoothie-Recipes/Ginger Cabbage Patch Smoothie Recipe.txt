Title: Ginger Cabbage Patch Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 cup roughly chopped cabbage
Ingredient: 1.0 cup red grapes
Ingredient: 1.0 red apple, cored and chopped
Ingredient: 1.0 large carrot, peeled and chopped
Ingredient: 0.5 cup water
Ingredient: 0.5 cup ice cubes
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon chopped fresh ginger
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend cabbage, grapes, apple, carrot, water, ice cubes, and ginger together in a blender until smooth.
