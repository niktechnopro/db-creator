Title: Orange Pear Shake Recipe

Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 pear, cored and chopped
Ingredient: 2.0 oranges, peeled and seeded
Ingredient: 0.25 cup milk
Ingredient: 0.75 cup vanilla ice cream
Directions: 
Duration: 5 m
Step: In a blender, combine pear, oranges, milk and ice cream. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
