Title: Very Orange Smoothie without Oranges Recipe

Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 mango - peeled, seeded, and chopped
Ingredient: 2.0 carrots, cut into chunks
Ingredient: 1.0 banana, chopped
Ingredient: 1.0 yellow bell pepper, chopped, or to taste
Ingredient: 0.5 red bell pepper, chopped, or more to taste
Ingredient: 1.0 ounce chilled mineral water, or as needed
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon lemon juice, or to taste
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon honey, or to taste (optional)
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon chia seeds, or to taste (optional)
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend mango, carrots, banana, yellow bell pepper, red bell pepper, mineral water, lemon juice, honey, or chia seeds in a blender until smooth. Add more water for a thinner consistency.
