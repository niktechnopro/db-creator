Title: Elvis Smoothie (Almond and Banana) Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/876280.jpg
Rating: 4.0
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 2.0 large ripe bananas, peeled and cut into chunks
Ingredient: 2.0 cups skim milk
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon almond butter
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon vanilla extract
Ingredient: 1.0 pinch ground cinnamon
Directions: 
Duration: 5 m
Step: Place banana chunks into a sealable plastic bag and freeze until solid, 1 to 2 hours to overnight.
Step: Place frozen bananas into a blender with skim milk, almond butter, and vanilla extract; blend until smooth. Pour into a large glass and garnish with a pinch of ground cinnamon to serve.
