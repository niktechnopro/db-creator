Title: Strawberry Lassi Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/381979.jpg
Rating: 4.53191471099854
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 9.0 ripe strawberries, rinsed
Ingredient: 2.0 tablespoons white sugar
Ingredient: 0.25 cup whole milk
Ingredient: 1.0 cup low-fat yogurt
Ingredient: 3.0 ice cubes
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Cut 3 strawberries into small pieces and set aside. In a blender, place the remaining strawberries and sugar; blend on high speed for 30 seconds. Pour in the milk and blend another 30 seconds. Add the yogurt and blend on high speed for 1 minute.
Step: To serve, place 1 ice cube in each of 3 glasses and pour in the lassi. Top with the strawberry pieces and serve.
