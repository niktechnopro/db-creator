Title: Cinnapear Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1015517.jpg
Rating: 4.55102062225342
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 2.0 pears, quartered and cores removed
Ingredient: 1.0 banana, cut in chunks
Ingredient: 1.0 cup milk
Ingredient: 0.5 cup vanilla yogurt
Ingredient: 0.5 teaspoon ground cinnamon
Ingredient: 1.0 pinch ground nutmeg
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Place the pears, banana, milk, yogurt, cinnamon, and nutmeg into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth. Pour into glasses to serve.
