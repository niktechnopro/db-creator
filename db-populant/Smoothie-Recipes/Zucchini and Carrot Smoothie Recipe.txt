Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 zucchini - peeled, grated, and chopped
Ingredient: 1.0 carrot - peeled, grated, and chopped
Ingredient: 0.5 cup plain fat-free Greek yogurt
Ingredient: 0.5 cup 1% milk
Ingredient: 2.0 tablespoons rolled oats
Ingredient: 2.0 tablespoons honey
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon flax seeds
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
