Title: Lela's Protein Mango Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2384098.jpg
Rating: 4.75
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 0.5 mango, chopped, or more to taste
Ingredient: 0.5 cup low-fat vanilla yogurt
Ingredient: 0.5 cup almond milk
Ingredient: 0.5 cup ice
Ingredient: 1.0 scoop vanilla whey protein powder
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon honey, or to taste (optional)
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend mango, yogurt, almond milk, ice, protein powder, and honey together in a blender until smooth.
