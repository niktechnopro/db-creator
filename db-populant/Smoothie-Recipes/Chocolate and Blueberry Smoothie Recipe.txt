Title: Chocolate and Blueberry Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/842436.jpg
Rating: 2.53333330154419
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 2.0 teaspoons cocoa powder
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon hot water
Ingredient: 1.0 cup milk
Ingredient: 4.0 tablespoons frozen blueberries
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon white sugar
Ingredient: 4.0 ice cubes
Directions: 
Duration: 5 m
Step: Mix together the cocoa powder and water in a small bowl until the cocoa is dissolved.
Step: Place the cocoa mixture, milk, blueberries, sugar, and ice cubes into a blender; cover and blend until smooth, 30 to 45 seconds. Serve cold.
