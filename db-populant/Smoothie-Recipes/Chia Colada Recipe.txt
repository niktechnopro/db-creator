Title: Chia Colada Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3292821.jpg
Rating: 3.75
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 cup coconut water
Ingredient: 2.0 tablespoons pineapple juice
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon chia seeds
Ingredient: 0.25 cup chopped pineapple
Directions: 
Duration: 5 m
Step: Blend coconut water, pineapple juice, and chia seeds together in a blender. Set mixture aside until chia seeds expand, about 30 minutes. Top mixture with chopped pineapple to serve.
