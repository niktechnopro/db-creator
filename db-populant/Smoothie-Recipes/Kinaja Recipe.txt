Title: Kinaja Recipe

Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 cup orange juice
Ingredient: 2.0 kiwis, peeled and sliced
Ingredient: 3.0 ripe bananas, sliced
Ingredient: 3.0 pineapple rings
Ingredient: 0.5 cup unsweetened pineapple juice
Ingredient: 2.0 cups crushed ice
Directions: 
Duration: 0 m
Step: In an electric blender, mix together orange juice, kiwi, bananas, pineapple, and pineapple juice until well blended. Pour the mixture into a pitcher.
Step: Place the ice in the blender, then pour the fruit mixture over the ice. Process until the ice is blended in with the fruit juices.
