Title: Coconut Banango Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 mango - peeled, seeded, and chopped
Ingredient: 1.0 frozen bananas, sliced
Ingredient: 0.5 cup coconut milk
Ingredient: 0.5 cup cold water
Ingredient: 4.0 cubes  ice, or as needed
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon white sugar
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon coconut extract
Ingredient: 0.5 teaspoon lime zest
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend mango, bananas, coconut milk, water, ice, sugar, coconut extract, and lime zest in a blender until smooth.
