Title: Vegan Smoothie Bowl with Carrot and Banana Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3314783.jpg
Rating: 5.0
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 2.0 pitted Medjool dates
Ingredient: 1.0 frozen banana, chopped
Ingredient: 1.0 cup coarsely chopped carrot
Ingredient: 0.5 cup unsweetened vanilla-flavored almond milk, or more to taste
Ingredient: 0.5 teaspoon ground cinnamon
Ingredient: 0.25 teaspoon ground ginger
Ingredient: 2.0 tablespoons flaked coconut
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon goji berries
Directions: 
Duration: 15 m
Step: Place dates in a small bowl and cover with cold water; let soak, about 5 minutes. Drain and chop.
Step: Place chopped dates, banana, carrot, almond milk, cinnamon, and ginger in a blender; puree until smoothie is thick and smooth. Pour into a serving bowl.
Step: Top smoothie bowl with flaked coconut and goji berries.
