Title: Quick Strawberry Oatmeal Breakfast Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1322171.jpg
Rating: 4.33333349227905
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 0.5 cup rolled oats
Ingredient: 1.0 teaspoon chia seeds
Ingredient: 14.0 frozen strawberries
Ingredient: 6.0 ounces nonfat vanilla Greek yogurt
Ingredient: 1.0 banana, broken into chunks
Ingredient: 0.5 cup almond milk
Ingredient: 0.5 teaspoon vanilla extract
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend oats and chia seeds together in a blender to a fine consistency. Add strawberries, yogurt, banana, almond milk, and vanilla extract; blend until smooth.
