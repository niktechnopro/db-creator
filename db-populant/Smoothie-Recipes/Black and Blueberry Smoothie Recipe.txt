Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 cup unsweetened almond milk
Ingredient: 0.5 cup frozen blackberries
Ingredient: 0.5 cup frozen blueberries
Ingredient: 2.0 tablespoons coconut butter
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon honey
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon chia seeds
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
