Title: Raw Mango Monster Smoothie Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2800715.jpg
Rating: 4.5
Ingredients: 
Ingredient: 1.0 tablespoon flax seeds
Ingredient: 2.0 tablespoons pepitas (raw pumpkin seeds)
Ingredient: 1.0 ripe mango, cubed
Ingredient: 1.0 frozen banana, quartered
Ingredient: 0.3333333333333333 cup water, or more to taste
Ingredient: 3.0 ice cubes
Ingredient: 2.0 leaves kale, or more to taste
Directions: 
Duration: 10 m
Step: Blend flax seeds in a blender until finely ground; add pepitas and blend until ground, about 1 minute.
Step: Place mango, banana, water, ice cubes, and kale in the blender; blend until smooth, kale is fully incorporated, and the smoothie is uniform in color, about 3 minutes. Thin with more water to reach desired consistency.
